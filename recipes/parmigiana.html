Ingredients

  • 600 grams of zucchini
  • 400 grams of smoked scamorza or well-drained mozzarella
  • 9 slices of cooked ham
  • approximately 6 tablespoons of Parmesan or Grana cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • fresh basil

How to make Zucchini Parmigiana:

First of all, thinly slice the zucchini using a mandolin slicer. Slice the zucchini - Zucchini Parmigiana Recipe Brush a baking dish with a drizzle of olive oil, then layer with zucchini slices, 3 slices of cooked ham, a portion of well-drained scamorza or mozzarella, and 2 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese.

Alternate zucchini, mozzarella, ham, and Parmesan - Zucchini Parmigiana Recipe Next, add a handful of basil and start layering again with zucchini, ham, scamorza, Parmesan, and basil. I made 3 stuffed layers. Finally, finish with a final layer of zucchini, diced scamorza, a sprinkle of Parmesan, and basil.
